# Open Questions

The following questions need human clarification before proceeding:

1. **Should employees be able to see their manager's leave requests, or only their own?**
2. Clarification needed: Do employees need visibility into their manager’s leave status?
3. **What is the exact timeline for launch and any regulatory approval requirements?**
4. Need confirmation on go-live date and whether internal compliance teams must approve system access or data handling procedures.
5. **Are there specific reporting dashboards required by management beyond standard metrics?**
6. Are there additional KPIs or visualizations that need to be built into the dashboard?
7. **Is there an existing HRIS system in place, and what is its architecture (e.g., cloud-based vs. on-prem)?**
8. Required for integration planning and data flow mapping.
9. **Will there be a phased rollout strategy, or full deployment at once?**
10. Impacts training approach, user adoption tracking, and rollback plans.
